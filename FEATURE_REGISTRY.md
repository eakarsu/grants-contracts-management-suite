## Grant RFP Proposal

- `grant-draft` - Grant Draft: Narrative sections, funder priorities, budget notes, attachments, and review status.
- `rfp-compliance-matrix` - RFP Compliance Matrix: Requirement mapping, response owners, evidence, gaps, and submission readiness.
- `sow-generator` - SOW Generator: Scope, deliverables, milestones, assumptions, pricing notes, and approval workflow.
- `proposal-submission-tracker` - Proposal Submission Tracker: Deadlines, forms, attachments, questions, reviewers, and final submit checklist.
